Frozen Strawberry Lemonade

Description

This frozen strawberry lemonade is a low calorie sweet drink perfect for the summer. Make a big batch for a summer party, or make one just for yourself as you bask in the sun.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Frozen Strawberries
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1/4 Cup Sweetener
  • High Powered Blender

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to the blender and close.
  2. Blend at high speed for 30-40 seconds.
  3. You're done! Just pour and serve.
  4. Yes it was that easy.
