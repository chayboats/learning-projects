Risotto Style Pasta

Description

Risotto style pasta is one of my favorite dishes. It's like a formula to make many different meals throughout the week. You can choose your own pasta, your own veggies, spices, cheeses, and more!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp: Oil - olive oil, butter, margarine; etc
  • 5 oz: Protein - chicken, bacon, ground meat; etc
  • Aromatics - garlic, onions, peppers
  • Veggies - carrots, broccoli, cauliflour; etc
  • 2 tbsp: Deglazer - white wine, stock; etc
  • 6oz: Pasta - penne, spaghetti, rigatonni; etc
  • Hot Liquid - water, chicken stock, tomato sauce, heavy cream; etc
  • Thickeners - cooking wine, corn starch slurry, flour; etc
  • Seasonings - garlic powder, onion powder, paprika; etc
  • (Optional) 1/4 cup: Cheeses - mozzarella, parmesean, colby; etc

Steps

  1. Add oil to a sauce pan over medium heat.
  2. Add in the protein and cook to the correct internal temperature.
  3. Remove protein from the pan leaving as much of the juices as possible.
  4. Lower the heat and add in your veggies and aromatics. Cook to your liking.
  5. Place the veggies and aromatics to the side with the protein.
  6. Raise the temperature, and use a deglazer to clean the bottom of the pan.
  7. Add your pasta to the pan, and add just enough liquid to cover the pasta.
  8. Stirring occassionally, cook pasta to your liking. (If the liquid evaportates before the pasta is finished, just add more liquid).
  9. Lower the temperature and add any thickeners to your sauce.
  10. Add any cheeses and season to taste.
