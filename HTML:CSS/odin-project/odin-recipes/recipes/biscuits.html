Homemade Honey Butter Biscuits

Description

Ever get a craving for sweet delicious carbs? Me too!
I was craving biscuits one day and scoured the internet for an easy recipe to follow. Lucky for you--I found it! I have made these biscuits 4 or 5 times, but there was one time that I was extremely proud of them. The biscuits always tasted delicious, but this one time I was able to get them fluffy to perfection!

Some things to keep in mind when making these biscuits:

  • Handle the dough with care: Mix the ingredients lightly. Press and fold the dough gently. If you're too
    rough the biscuits will come out dense and dry.
  • Keep it cold: If you notice your ingredients getting a bit warm, stick the dough in the fridge for a bit.
    Cold ingredients helps with the fluffiness!

Ingredients

Biscuits

  • Flour - 2 cups (Plus extra for sprinkling)
  • Baking Powder - 1 tbsp
  • Baking Soda - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Frozen Unsalted Butter - 7 tbsp
  • Cold Buttermilk - 1 cup

Honey-Butter Glaze

  • Unsalted Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Honey - 1/2 tbsp
  • Sugar - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1/8 tsp

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 415° F.
  2. Prepare a round or square pan with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together baking powder, baking soda, 2 cups of flour, 1/2 tsp of salt, and 2 tbsp of sugar.
  4. Sift these ingredients into another bowl.
  5. Shred 7 tbsp of butter into the mixture.
  6. Use a pastry cutter or fork to cut the butter into the flour mixture.
  7. Pour in the cold buttermilk.
  8. Set out a sheet of parchment paper (or dough mat) on the counter and sprinkle the extra flour onto the surface.
  9. Mix the ingredients in the bowl GENTLY with hands. (It should be VERY sticky)
  10. Move the dough onto the prepared surface.
  11. Flour your hands then GENTLY press out and fold the dough.
  12. Repeat step 11 about 6 times.
  13. Cut out the dough using a cookie cutter, cup, or mason jar lid.
  14. Place biscuits close to one another in the prepared pan. (This helps with rising.)
  15. Warm the honey and butter for the glaze on the stove or in the microwave.
  16. Add the salt and sugar then mix and brush onto the biscuits.
  17. Bake the biscuits at 415° F for 10-20 mins or until golden brown.
  18. Brush again with the glaze and serve warm!
